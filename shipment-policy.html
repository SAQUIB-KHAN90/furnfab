Shipment Policy

Furnfabinterio is committed to delivering your furniture safely and efficiently across Pune and other parts of India.

Delivery Timelines

  • Standard delivery within Pune: 3-5 business days.
  • Interstate delivery: 7-14 business days, depending on location.
  • Customized orders may have extended delivery timelines, which will be communicated at the time of order confirmation.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges vary based on product size, weight, and delivery location. Free shipping is available on select products and during promotional periods.

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your shipment's progress.

Contact Us
